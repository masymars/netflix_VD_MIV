Exploring Netflix TV Shows and Series

Welcome to an interactive scrollytelling experience that explores Netflix's vast collection of TV shows and series. This demo utilizes scrollama.js and d3.js to guide you through interesting insights and highlights from the data. Ensure your screen is at least 1100 pixels wide for the best experience. The demo has been optimized for modern browsers, with a focus on Firefox. Technical notes are provided below.

Netflix Film Production

Netflix invested heavily in film production until 2019, when the pandemic led to a decline. The global crisis significantly impacted the industry, prompting a shift in Netflix's strategy.

Movies VS TV shows

Notably, Netflix's investment in content favored movies over TV shows. The cost-effectiveness of producing movies became a key factor, leading the streaming giant to allocate more resources to film production. Movies, TV shows.

Production by Country

Netflix's film production is spread across different countries, with a significant focus on the USA, India, and the UK. This global distribution is influenced by the dominance of Hollywood in the United States and Bollywood in India. The rich cinematic traditions of these regions contribute to Netflix's diverse and vibrant content portfolio. USA, India, UK.

Genre of Shows

In the realm of genres, foreign films and TV shows take the lead in Netflix's diverse content library, owing to the vast number produced globally. This international approach adds a rich cultural tapestry to the platform's offerings. Notably, within the United States, comedy and drama emerge as the dominant genres, with a prolific output in the creation of movies and shows.
